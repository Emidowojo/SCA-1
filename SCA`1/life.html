Early Life

He was born on August 23, 1978, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, to Joe Bryant and Pamela Cox Bryant. He has two elder sisters named Shaya and Sharia. He played basketball and football from an early age and was a fan of the ‘Los Angeles Lakers’ and the football team ‘AC Milan.’ When he was six years old, his family shifted to Rieti, Italy. In 1991, his family returned to Philadelphia and Kobe enrolled at the ‘Lower Merion High School.’ His participation in basketball won the ‘Lower Merion High School’ a state championship after 53 years

This remarkable player was named ‘NBA Most Valuable Player’ in 2008. He has won the ‘NBA Finals MVP’ on two occasions. He led his team to victory in the ‘NBA Finals’ on five occasions. Bryant was named the ‘NBA All-Star’ on 18 occasions. He was also named ‘NBA All-Star Game MVP’ on four occasions In 2001, he married Vanessa Laine and the couple have four daughters, Natalia, Gianna, Bianka, and Capri. In October 2018, Bryant published his book ‘The Mamba Mentality: How I Play’ which chronicles his career. Kobe Bryant, along with his 13-year-old daughter, Gianna Bryant, died on January 26, 2020, near Calabasas, California, in a helicopter crash.
